Simplicity is the key.

Private

All transactions with FIMFL are concealed. Sources/amounts transferred are hidden and encrypted, allowing high resistance from blockchain analysis.

Decentralized

With the use of ring signatures, authorization is given to a single unknown entity within a group of people. This allows anonimity and prevention of centralized authority.

Untraceable

With the use of ring signatures, a message is endorsed by someone in a particular group of people. This endorser is concealed as it is computationally infeasible to determine which key was used from the group of people. Ring signatures also do not allow the anonimity of individual signatures to be revoked.
